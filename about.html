About Corsivo

Words, beautifully slanted.

Welcome to Corsivo! This project holds a special place in my development journey: it is a modern revival of my very first repository on GitHub. Rebuilt from the ground up, it features a glassmorphic dark design system, fluid slanted text micro-animations, and full offline caching compatibility.

The name Corsivo (Italian for italic or cursive) highlights our goal to present word definitions with exquisite typography, visual transitions, and custom layout setups.

What Makes Corsivo Special

  • Multiple Dictionary Sources: Smart Search chains Free Dictionary, Wiktionary, and Datamuse APIs to deliver comprehensive definitions with intelligent fallback.
  • Auto Language Detection: Type in any supported language and Corsivo automatically detects the script — Arabic, Hindi, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Russian, and more.
  • Offline-First Design: Service Worker caching ensures your recently searched words are available even without internet connectivity.
  • Study Tools: Flashcards, vocabulary notebooks, and a word explorer help you build and retain your vocabulary over time.
  • Zero Dependencies: Built entirely with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no bloat, just fast, clean code.
